📚 About NotesHub

NotesHub is a modern platform designed to help B.Tech students easily access, share, and download academic notes in a structured way.

Our mission is to simplify learning by providing all essential study materials in one place, making education more accessible and efficient.

🚀 What We Offer

  • 📘 Semester-wise notes organization
  • 📄 Previous year questions (PYQs)
  • ⬇ Easy download & view system
  • 🔍 Smart subject-based navigation
  • 📱 Mobile-friendly experience

🎯 Our Vision

We aim to build a community-driven platform where students can contribute and access quality educational resources anytime, anywhere.
