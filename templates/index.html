📚 About NGF Knowledge Hub

NGF Knowledge Hub is a modern educational platform designed specifically for BTech students. We provide high-quality, semester-wise notes, subject materials, and academic resources for branches like Computer Science (CSE), Mechanical (ME), and Electronics (ECE).

Our mission is to create a centralized ecosystem where students can learn faster, access reliable study materials, and collaborate by sharing notes. The platform is structured to simplify complex topics and improve exam preparation efficiency.

🚀 Why Choose Our Platform?

  • ✔ Structured semester-wise notes
  • ✔ Easy and fast access to study material
  • ✔ Free downloads with no hidden charges
  • ✔ Community-driven learning (upload & share notes)
  • ✔ Mobile-friendly and fast-loading design
